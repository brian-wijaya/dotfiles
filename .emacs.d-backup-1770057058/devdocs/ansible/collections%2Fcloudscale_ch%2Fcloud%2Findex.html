These are the plugins in the cloudscale_ch.cloud collection:

  • custom_image module – Manage custom images on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • floating_ip module – Manages floating IPs on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • load_balancer module – Manages load balancers on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • load_balancer_health_monitor module – Manages load balancers on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • load_balancer_listener module – Manages load balancer listeners on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • load_balancer_pool module – Manages load balancer pools on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • load_balancer_pool_member module – Manages load balancer pool members on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • network module – Manages networks on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • objects_user module – Manages objects users on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • server module – Manages servers on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • server_group module – Manages server groups on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • subnet module – Manages subnets on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
  • volume module – Manages volumes on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service.
  • volume_snapshot module – Manage volume snapshots on the cloudscale.ch IaaS service
