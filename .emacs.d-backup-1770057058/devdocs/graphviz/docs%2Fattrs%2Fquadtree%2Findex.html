quadtree

Quadtree scheme to use

type: quadType | bool, default: normal

  • quadtree=true aliases quadtree=normal
  • quadtree=false aliases quadtree=none
  • quadtree=2 aliases quadtree=fast
Valid on:
  • Graphs

Note: sfdp only.
