epsilon

Terminating condition

type: double, default: .0001 * # nodes (mode == KK) , .0001 (mode == major) , .01 (mode == sgd)

If the length squared of all energy gradients are less than epsilon, the algorithm stops.

Valid on:
  • Graphs

Note: neato only.
